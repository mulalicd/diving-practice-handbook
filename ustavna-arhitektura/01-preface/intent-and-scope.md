# Intent and Scope

The purpose of this Handbook is to guide recreational divers toward a professional and ethical approach to SCUBA diving.  
It aims to restore the forgotten foundations of diving knowledge, emphasizing critical thinking, self-awareness, and safety.  

The scope includes:  
- Principles of diving safety and risk management  
- Forgotten foundations such as dive tables and decompression thinking  
- Practical examples and exercises to develop judgment and discipline  
- Ethical considerations and cultural awareness in diving  
- Tools, not rules: empowering divers to make informed decisions  

This Handbook is intended for recreational divers, instructors, and dive enthusiasts who seek to go beyond commercialized dive training and deepen their understanding of diving fundamentals.
