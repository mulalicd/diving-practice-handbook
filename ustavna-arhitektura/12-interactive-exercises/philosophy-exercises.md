## Exercise 1: Pre-Dive Self-Assessment

### Scenario
You're planning a 30m dive in cold water with a new buddy...

### Reflection Questions
1. What physical signs indicate I'm ready/not ready?
2. What concerns am I avoiding acknowledging?
3. How well do I know my buddy's capabilities?

### Self-Scoring Matrix
[Physical Readiness: 1-5]
[Mental Readiness: 1-5]
[Equipment Confidence: 1-5]

### Decision Framework
If total score < 12: Postpone or modify dive
If any individual score < 3: Address that specific concern
