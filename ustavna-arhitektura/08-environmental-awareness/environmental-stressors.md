# Environmental Stressors

Environmental stressors affect diver performance, safety, and enjoyment.

## Common Stressors
- Cold, heat, and currents  
- Low visibility or confined spaces  
- Marine life and underwater hazards  

## Managing Stress
- Pre-dive briefings and mental preparation  
- Gradual exposure to challenging conditions  
- Consistent practice of emergency skills  

## Key Takeaways
Awareness and preparation for environmental stressors enhance safety and confidence underwater.
