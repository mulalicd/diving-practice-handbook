# Currents and Visibility

Currents and visibility are critical environmental factors that affect dive safety.

## Currents
- Identify current strength and direction before dive  
- Plan entry and exit points  
- Maintain team cohesion and buddy proximity  

## Visibility
- Poor visibility increases disorientation and collision risk  
- Use reference lines or reels in low-visibility dives  
- Employ lights strategically for navigation and communication  

## Planning Guidelines
- Always have contingency plans for unexpected environmental changes  
- Use surface support when conditions are challenging  

## Key Takeaways
Currents and visibility must be actively managed through planning and teamwork.
