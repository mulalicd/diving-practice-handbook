# Mental Checklists

Mental checklists are cognitive tools that support safe diving practices.

## Why They Matter
- Reduce human error under stress  
- Help maintain focus on critical safety tasks  
- Reinforce habits through repetition  

## Examples
- Pre-dive mental walkthroughs  
- Equipment redundancy checks  
- Emergency scenario rehearsals  

## Key Takeaways
Mental checklists complement physical checks, enhancing safety and confidence in diving operations.
