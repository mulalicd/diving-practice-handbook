# Risk Matrix for Diving

A risk matrix allows divers to visualize and quantify potential hazards.

## Using a Risk Matrix
- Assess probability and severity of risks  
- Categorize risks as low, medium, or high  
- Develop mitigation strategies for each risk level  

## Practical Tips
- Regularly update matrix based on dive site and team experience  
- Encourage team discussion and consensus  
- Use matrix outcomes to inform Go / No-Go decisions  

## Key Takeaways
A risk matrix is a practical tool to structure risk assessment, improve decision-making, and reduce preventable incidents.
