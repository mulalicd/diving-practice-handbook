# Stress and Task Loading

High stress or excessive task demands increase the likelihood of errors.

## Sources of Stress
- Environmental: poor visibility, strong currents, cold water.  
- Equipment: unfamiliar gear, malfunctions, or complex configurations.  
- Psychological: fear, fatigue, or anxiety.  

## Task Management
- Prioritize essential tasks and avoid multitasking under pressure.  
- Use pre-dive planning to reduce cognitive load.  
- Support team members by sharing responsibilities and maintaining communication.  

## Key Takeaways
Recognizing stress and managing task load are critical to safe, competent diving.
