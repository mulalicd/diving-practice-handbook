# Student Protection

Protecting students is a core ethical responsibility of all dive professionals.

## Key Protection Strategies
- Clear communication of risks and expectations  
- Supervised practice with appropriate ratios  
- Strict adherence to safety protocols and emergency preparedness  

## Mental and Emotional Safety
- Respect individual limits and stress tolerance  
- Create a supportive learning environment  
- Encourage reporting of concerns or incidents  

## Key Takeaways
Student protection is both a legal and moral obligation for instructors and dive operators.
