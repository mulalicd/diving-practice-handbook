# Instructor Responsibility

Diving instructors carry significant responsibility for the safety and development of their students.

## Professional Duties
- Maintain up-to-date certification and knowledge  
- Provide accurate, clear, and unbiased instruction  
- Model ethical behavior at all times  

## Student-Centered Approach
- Adapt teaching methods to individual needs  
- Encourage self-awareness and self-reflection  
- Promote lifelong learning and safe diving habits
