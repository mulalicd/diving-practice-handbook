# Planning and Discipline in Diving

Safe and enjoyable dives are built on careful planning and disciplined execution.

## Dive Planning
- **Set clear objectives**: Know the purpose and expected conditions of the dive.  
- **Check equipment thoroughly**: Inspect regulators, BCDs, and tanks before every dive.  
- **Calculate gas requirements**: Include reserve and contingencies.  
- **Plan depth and bottom time**: Respect limits from dive tables or computer algorithms.  

## Discipline Underwater
- Maintain constant awareness of depth, air supply, and environment.  
- Follow planned ascent rates and safety stops.  
- Avoid distractions or unnecessary risks.  

## Key Takeaways
Discipline is the diver's most reliable safety tool. Technology aids planning but cannot replace focus and responsibility.
