# Team Diving Reality

Diving is often a team activity; understanding team dynamics is essential.

## Principles of Team Diving
- Pre-dive briefing: Assign roles, review signals, and discuss objectives.  
- Stay within visual or tactile contact at all times.  
- Share responsibility: Watch for signs of stress, equipment issues, or disorientation.  

## Handling Emergencies
- Maintain calm and follow predetermined emergency procedures.  
- Support each other with air-sharing, navigation, or surface assistance.  
- Debrief after emergencies to improve future performance.  

## Key Takeaways
Team cohesion and mutual responsibility enhance safety and enjoyment for all participants.
