# Situational Assessment

Recognizing and assessing situational risks is a cornerstone of safe diving.

## Environmental Assessment
- Monitor currents, visibility, water temperature, and potential hazards.  
- Adjust dive plan according to real-time conditions.  

## Personal Assessment
- Check your physical and mental state before and during the dive.  
- Be honest about fatigue, anxiety, or other limitations.  

## Team Assessment
- Communicate continuously with your dive buddy or team.  
- Ensure everyone is aware of plan changes and safety measures.  

## Key Takeaways
Situational awareness prevents accidents and strengthens decision-making underwater.
