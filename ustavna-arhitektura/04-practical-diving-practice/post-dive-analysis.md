# Post-Dive Analysis

Reflecting after each dive consolidates learning and improves future safety.

## Self-Review
- Note any equipment issues or malfunctions.  
- Record personal feelings, stress, or fatigue levels.  
- Review gas usage and time management.  

## Team Review
- Discuss observations with dive buddies.  
- Evaluate team communication and coordination.  
- Identify errors, near-misses, and areas for improvement.  

## Documentation
- Maintain a dive log for future reference.  
- Include notes on environmental conditions and hazards.  
- Track progress on skills, experience, and confidence.  

## Key Takeaways
Reflection transforms each dive into a learning opportunity, building competence and resilience.
