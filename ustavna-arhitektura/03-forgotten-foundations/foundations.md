# Forgotten Foundations of Diving

This chapter revisits the fundamental principles every diver should know:

- Understanding pressure, buoyancy, and gas behavior underwater.  
- Manual dive planning techniques and the use of dive tables.  
- Gas laws and their practical implications.  
- How foundational knowledge reduces reliance on electronics and increases safety.

Mastering these basics empowers divers to **think critically and respond effectively** in any underwater scenario.
