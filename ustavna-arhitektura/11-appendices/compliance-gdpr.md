# GDPR Compliance

Ensuring data protection and privacy is critical for any digital diving handbook.

## Key Principles
- Collect only necessary data  
- Obtain explicit consent from users  
- Secure storage and encryption of personal data  
- Provide clear privacy policies and user rights  

## Implementation in the Web App
- Track reading progress anonymously or with user consent  
- Encrypt any personal identifiers  
- Regularly audit and update privacy measures  

## Key Takeaways
GDPR compliance builds trust with users and ensures legal adherence while maintaining safe and ethical handling of data.
