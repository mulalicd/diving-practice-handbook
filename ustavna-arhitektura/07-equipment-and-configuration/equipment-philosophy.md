# Equipment Philosophy

Equipment should enhance safety, efficiency, and comfort, not compensate for poor skills.

## Selection Criteria
- Suitability for the planned environment  
- Reliability and maintenance history  
- Personal comfort and familiarity  

## Mindset
- Avoid over-reliance on technology  
- Understand fundamental diving principles behind every tool  
- Practice with each piece until usage is instinctive
